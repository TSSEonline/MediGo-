ABOUT MEDIGO

Who We Are

MediGo Couriers LLC is a professional medical courier company providing safe, secure, and timely transport of medical materials. We specialize in compliant deliveries for healthcare providers, laboratories, clinics, and government agencies.

We operate in full HIPAA compliance, ensuring secure and confidential handling of all medical items through strict chain-of-custody procedures. MediGo Couriers LLC adheres to all applicable federal and state regulations governing the transport of Protected Health Information (PHI) and is fully insured to provide added protection and peace of mind for our clients.
